About Our Company

Learn about our journey of delivering premium Indian exports to global markets

Our Story

Indian Raw Goods Export has grown from a small family business to a leading exporter of premium Indian products worldwide. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a trusted partner for businesses across 35+ countries.

We specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting the finest quality dehydrated foods, crops, powders, spices, and pharmaceutical ingredients from various regions of India.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous quality controls and testing at every stage of production

Ethical Sourcing

Fair partnerships with farmers and sustainable agricultural practices

Global Compliance

Meeting international standards and certification requirements

Our Mission

To deliver the highest quality Indian raw goods to global markets while ensuring ethical sourcing, sustainable practices, and exceeding customer expectations through reliability and excellence.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted global ambassador of premium Indian exports, setting industry standards for quality, sustainability, and customer service while supporting fair trade and local communities.

Our Journey

1998

Company Founded

Indian Raw Goods Export was established as a small family business focusing on exporting spices.

2003

Product Line Expansion

Expanded our product range to include dehydrated vegetables and fruits.

2008

International Growth

Established partnerships in 15 countries and opened our first international office.

2012

Quality Certification

Achieved ISO 22000 certification for our quality management systems.

2017

Sustainable Farming Initiative

Launched our sustainable farming program to support ethical and eco-friendly practices.

2023

Global Leader

Now serving 35+ countries as a trusted supplier of premium Indian exports worldwide.
