Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for your products?

Minimum order quantities vary by product category. For most items, we require a minimum order of 100 kg, but this can vary for specialized products. Please contact our sales team for specific product MOQs.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products carry various certifications including ISO 22000, HACCP, Organic, Kosher, and Halal certifications. The specific certifications available vary by product and can be provided upon request.

What are your shipping terms?

We offer various shipping terms including FOB, CIF, and EXW. The specific terms can be negotiated based on your requirements and the destination country. We work with reliable logistics partners to ensure timely delivery.

Can you provide custom packaging for your products?

Yes, we offer custom packaging solutions to meet your specific requirements. This includes custom pack sizes, private labeling, and specialized packaging materials for extended shelf life or market-specific needs.

How do you ensure product quality and safety?

We maintain strict quality control measures throughout our supply chain. This includes supplier verification, raw material testing, in-process controls, and finished product analysis. Our facilities adhere to international quality and food safety standards, and we provide detailed Certificates of Analysis with each shipment.
